Project 4

Poster for Japanese Sake

Product design

I created this poster for the Illustration for New Media class. I undertook the entire process, starting from designing the logo for the product to the final poster. I brainstormed on how to make the product appealing, particularly focusing on illustrating the plums used in this sake with Adobe Illustrator. This was done to convey the sweet and flavorful image associated with the product. The task was completed using Illustrator and Photoshop.
